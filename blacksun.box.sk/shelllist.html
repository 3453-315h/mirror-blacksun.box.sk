What is a UNIX shell account ?


A so-called 'shell' account is a terminal-type account on a Unix server. A shell is basically a program that is interactive and accepts commands from the user. By using telnet, rlogin, ssh, and a valid username and password you can gain access to the Unix machine.When you gain access, your computer acts as a remote terminal on the Unix machine. The Unix machine writes on your terminal screen and accepts input from your keyboard. The  shell environment is a very  powerful internet tool, for programmers, unix guru's and just plain users.
