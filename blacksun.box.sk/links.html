Info about mirroring our tutorials:
If you want to mirror some of tutorials we advise you not to do that, just make a link to them on our server. That's because some tutorials have been rewritten, and some of them will be :-) So the best thing to do is to link them, that way you will always have the newest versions. There were few problems in the past with that, some sites had outdated versions of our tutorials and there was lots of misunderstandings... That's why you should listen to us, because we are always right :-)
