Byte Me

This section is made up of mini-tutorials that R a v e n used to send using our mailing list, back in the old days when he had more time to work on it. The name comes from the original byte-me corner in our ex-daily magazine.He also used to send trivia questions, so we'll put these as well, together with their answers.
Note: we are also adding new material that wasn't sent out through the mailing list.  Why the name byte-me, you ask? Well, some say it's because it contains lots of small chunks or "bytes" of data. Some say it's designed for people who aren't into reading long tutorials, so they just say "bite me" to everyone and go here. Some say that looking back to it, it was a really stupid name. That's me.
